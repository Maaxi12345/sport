💥 1. Core Plyometrics (4-Week Foundation)

Build lower-body elasticity and explosive power to improve movement economy. Do twice a week.

Week 1: Foundation & Ankle Stiffness

ExerciseSets x RepsKey Focus
Pogo Jumps ↗3 x 15Keep knees relatively straight; bounce using just your calves.
Squat Jumps ↗3 x 8Sink into a half squat, explode up, and land softly.
Drop Freezes ↗3 x 5Step off a low box (12in) and stick the landing. Do not jump.
⏱ Rest 90 seconds between all sets.

Week 2: Force Absorption

ExerciseSets x RepsKey Focus
Pogo Jumps ↗3 x 20Faster ground contact times than Week 1.
Broad Jumps ↗3 x 5Jump forward max distance. Reset completely between reps.
Lateral Bounds ↗3 x 8 / legJump side-to-side from one foot to the other.
⏱ Rest 2 minutes between broad jumps and bounds.

Week 3: Elasticity & Speed

ExerciseSets x RepsKey Focus
Tuck Jumps ↗3 x 6Jump high, bring knees to chest, react immediately on landing.
Box Jumps ↗4 x 5Explode onto box. Step down carefully.
Continuous Broad ↗3 x 4Link jumps together. As soon as you land, launch again.

Week 4: Integration & Power

ExerciseSets x RepsKey Focus
Single-Leg Hops ↗3 x 5 / legHop forward on one leg. Focus on stability when you land.
Box Jumps ↗4 x 6Increase box height slightly from last week.
Depth Jumps ↗3 x 4Step off box, hit ground, immediately jump max vertical.

⚡ 2. Core Sprinting (4-Week Velocity)

Develop acceleration, mechanics, and top-end speed. Do 1-2 times a week on non-plyo days.

Week 1: Mechanics & Strides

ExerciseSets x DistanceKey Focus
A-Skips ↗3 x 20mHigh knees, strike ground directly under center of mass.
Strides ↗4 x 60mAccelerate to ~80% of max speed, hold, then glide to a stop.
Short Hill Sprints ↗4 x 10 secModerate incline. Focus on aggressive arm drive.

Week 2: Acceleration Phase

ExerciseSets x DistanceKey Focus
Wall Drills ↗3 x 10 secLean against wall at 45 degrees. Drive knees like pistons.
Flying 20s ↗4 x 60m total20m build-up → 20m max sprint → 20m deceleration.
Hill Sprints ↗6 x 10 secSlightly steeper incline than last week. 95% effort.

Week 3: Speed Endurance

ExerciseSets x DistanceKey Focus
B-Skips ↗3 x 20mExtend leg forward and actively "paw" the ground.
100m Repeats ↗4 x 100m90% effort. Keep shoulders relaxed.
150m Repeats ↗2 x 150m85% effort. Do not let form break down in last 30m.

Week 4: Max Velocity

ExerciseSets x DistanceKey Focus
Bounding ↗3 x 20mExaggerated steps maximizing air time and distance.
50m Max Effort ↗5 x 50m100% all-out effort. Run completely through finish line.
Flying 30s ↗3 x 70m total20m build-up → 30m absolute max speed → 20m decel.
⏱ Rest 3 to 4 minutes between sets. Your CNS needs time to reset.
