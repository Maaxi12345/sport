4-Week Plyometrics Training Plan

Build lower-body elasticity and explosive power to improve your movement economy.

Training Guidelines:
  • Do this routine twice a week.
  • Leave at least 48 hours of recovery between sessions.
  • Always start with 5–10 minutes of dynamic warm-ups (leg swings, lunges, high knees) before jumping.

Week 1: Foundation & Ankle Stiffness

Goal: Get joints used to impact and minimize foot ground-contact time.

Exercise Sets x Reps Focus
Pogo Jumps ↗ 3 x 15 Keep knees relatively straight; bounce using just your calves and ankles.
Squat Jumps ↗ 3 x 8 Sink into a half squat, explode up, and land softly.
Drop Freezes ↗ 3 x 5 Step off a low box (12 inches) and "stick" the landing. Do not jump off; just drop.
⏱ Rest 90 seconds between all sets

Week 2: Force Absorption

Goal: Increase horizontal load to build explosive power and decelerate effectively.

Exercise Sets x Reps Focus
Pogo Jumps ↗ 3 x 20 Faster ground contact times than Week 1.
Broad Jumps ↗ 3 x 5 Jump forward as far as possible. Reset completely between each rep.
Lateral Bounds ↗ 3 x 8 / leg Jump side-to-side from one foot to the other (like a speed skater).
⏱ Rest 2 minutes between broad jumps and lateral bounds

Week 3: Elasticity & Speed

Goal: Transition into "true" plyometrics, focusing on the stretch-shortening cycle.

Exercise Sets x Reps Focus
Tuck Jumps ↗ 3 x 6 Jump high, bring knees to chest, and react immediately upon landing.
Box Jumps ↗ 4 x 5 Explode onto a sturdy box. Step down carefully—never jump backward off a box.
Continuous Broad Jumps ↗ 3 x 4 Link the jumps together. As soon as you land, launch into the next one.
⏱ Rest 2 minutes between all sets. Quality over quantity.

Week 4: Integration & Power

Goal: Combine vertical, horizontal, and single-leg power. Stop if form breaks down.

Exercise Sets x Reps Focus
Single-Leg Hops ↗ 3 x 5 / leg Hop forward on one leg. Focus on stability when you land.
Box Jumps ↗ 4 x 6 Increase the box height slightly if the previous week felt easy.
Depth Jumps ↗ 3 x 4 Step off a low box, hit the ground, and immediately jump vertically as high as you can.
⏱ Rest 2 to 3 minutes between sets. Your central nervous system needs it.
