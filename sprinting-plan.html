4-Week Sprinting Plan

Develop acceleration, running mechanics, and top-end velocity.

Training Guidelines:
  • Do this 1-2 times a week, ideally on non-plyometric days.
  • Warm-up is non-negotiable: 10 mins light jogging followed by dynamic stretches (leg swings, walking lunges).
  • Sprinting is about speed, not fatigue. If you feel yourself slowing down heavily, end the workout.

Week 1: Mechanics & Strides

Goal: Prime the nervous system and dial in your running form without pushing to 100% max effort yet.

Exercise Sets x Distance Focus
A-Skips ↗ 3 x 20m High knees, striking the ground directly under your center of mass.
Strides 4 x 60m Accelerate smoothly to ~80% of your max speed, hold it, then glide to a stop.
Short Hill Sprints ↗ 4 x 10 seconds Find a moderate incline. Focus on aggressive arm drive and knee lift.
⏱ Rest 2 minutes between Strides, and walk slowly down the hill for recovery on sprints.

Week 2: Acceleration Phase

Goal: Build explosive power from a dead stop and improve your drive phase.

Exercise Sets x Distance Focus
Wall Drills ↗ 3 x 10 seconds Lean against a wall at 45 degrees. Drive knees aggressively like a piston.
Flying 20s ↗ 4 x 60m total 20m build-up → 20m max sprint → 20m smooth deceleration.
Hill Sprints ↗ 6 x 10 seconds Slightly steeper incline than last week. 95% effort.
⏱ Rest 2 to 3 minutes between Flying 20s to ensure full ATP (energy) recovery.

Week 3: Speed Endurance

Goal: Learn to maintain your form and stay relaxed while running at high speeds over longer distances.

Exercise Sets x Distance Focus
B-Skips ↗ 3 x 20m Similar to A-skips, but extend the leg forward and actively "paw" the ground.
100m Repeats ↗ 4 x 100m Run at 90% effort. Focus on keeping your shoulders relaxed and breathing steady.
150m Repeats ↗ 2 x 150m Hold 85% effort. Do not let your form break down in the last 30 meters.
⏱ Walk back to the start line slowly between reps (approx. 3-4 minutes rest).

Week 4: Max Velocity

Goal: Pure top-end speed. Short distances, absolute maximum effort, and massive recovery periods.

Exercise Sets x Distance Focus
Bounding ↗ 3 x 20m Exaggerated running steps maximizing air time and forward distance per step.
50m Max Effort ↗ 5 x 50m 100% all-out effort from a static start. Run completely through the finish line.
Flying 30s ↗ 3 x 70m total 20m build-up → 30m absolute max speed → 20m deceleration.
⏱ Rest 3 to 4 minutes between all sets. Do not rush this; your CNS needs time to reset for max output.
